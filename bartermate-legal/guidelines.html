Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2025

BarterMate is built on generosity, fairness, and mutual respect. To keep our community safe and positive:

✅ Do:

  • Trade fairly and honestly
  • Be polite and professional in messages
  • Report inappropriate content

❌ Don’t:

  • Post illegal or offensive items
  • Spam or scam other users
  • Harass or intimidate anyone

Violations

Accounts breaking these guidelines may be suspended or banned.
